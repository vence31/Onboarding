Master Service Agreement

StayVCS LLC · Legal Marketing Platform · 2026

1. Nature of Services

StayVCS LLC ("Company") operates a legal marketing and intake qualification platform. Services provided are limited to marketing data, case scoring analysis, and dashboard access. Company does not provide legal advice, does not practice law, and is not a law firm.

2. No Referral Fee / Fee Splitting

All fees charged by StayVCS LLC are flat subscription fees for software access and marketing data services only. No fee is contingent upon, calculated as a percentage of, or in any way connected to legal fees earned by the subscribing firm. This structure is designed to comply with ABA Model Rule 7.2 and Rule 1.5(e). StayVCS LLC never takes a percentage of any legal fee or case settlement.

3. State Priority Exclusivity

Upon activation, the subscribing firm receives priority lead routing for the selected state(s) for the duration of their active subscription. Company agrees not to grant priority routing for the same state to another firm while the subscription remains active. Standard (non-priority) access may remain available to other firms.

4. Data Ownership

StayVCS LLC retains ownership of the scoring algorithm, dashboard technology, and platform infrastructure. The subscribing firm owns the attorney-client relationship upon independent initiation of contact with any claimant. Claimant data provided through the dashboard is licensed for use in evaluating potential representation only.

5. Independent Review

The subscribing attorney acknowledges that all case scoring data is provided for informational purposes only. The firm is solely responsible for conducting its own independent evaluation of each case and making all intake decisions. StayVCS LLC makes no warranty regarding case eligibility, likelihood of approval, or case value.

6. Compliance Acknowledgment

The subscribing attorney warrants that they are a licensed attorney in good standing in the state(s) for which they are activating access, and that their use of this platform complies with all applicable state bar rules and professional responsibility obligations.

7. Subscription Terms

Subscriptions are billed monthly. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. No refunds are issued for partial months. Company reserves the right to modify pricing with 30 days written notice.

8. Limitation of Liability

StayVCS LLC's liability is limited to the amount paid in the prior 30-day subscription period. Company is not liable for missed cases, lost revenue, or decisions made based on platform data.
